Guidelines for IT (PRACTICAL) Assignment Copy - Semester III

  1. Hard Bound Practical Lab File (WITHOUT shoelace), to be used.

  2. On the first page, mention the following details:

    • Name of Candidate
    • Semester
    • CU Roll Number (used in Semester II examination)
    • CU Registration Number
    • College UID

  3. Write the questions as given in the assignment followed by their steps of solving on the ruled portion and take the screenshots of output pages and take color printouts.

  4. Cut and paste the printouts on the white pages corresponding to the questions/solution steps.

  5. Cover your assignment copy with brown paper.
